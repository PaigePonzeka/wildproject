Heather Phelps-Lipton

Heather Phelps-Lipton was born in Ithaca, N.Y. and raised by wolves. Her photography is a dialogue between curiosity and alienation and explores the drama of the everyday.

Heather studied art at San Diego State, technique at ICP and collodion under Jill Eisenberg and Joni Sternbach. Mostly working in medium-format and digital, she also shoots panoramically and through the viewfinder. Her photographs have been shown in San Diego, LA and New York. In addition, she has shown photo-based pieces that employ pencil, crayon, embroidery and projection. Publishing credits include the NY Times, NY Magazine, Village Voice, Time Out NY, Dutch, Brooklyn Edible, Japanese Vogue, Guns, Luna, Nona Brooklyn and Newsday. Heather lives/works in Red Hook, Brooklyn, with her partner, two dogs and a cat.
