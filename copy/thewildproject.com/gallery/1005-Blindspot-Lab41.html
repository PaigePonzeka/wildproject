We are pleased to invite you to the

Blind Spot Lab & Exhibition for Issue 41

Curated by Dana Faconti,
Gemma Hart Ingalls,
Jodie Vicenta Jacobson
& Glynnis McDaris


May 21 – June 26, 2010
Exhibition has been extended through August 28, 2010

Opening Reception:
Friday, May 21st, 6:30–9:30 pm

Featuring an exhibition, installations, films, and performances by

  • Davide Balula
  • Constance De Jong
  • Dru Donovan
  • Chris Gentile
  • Corin Hewitt
  • David La Spina
  • Babette Mangolte
  • Susanne Winterling



Gallery Hours:
Thursday/Friday 1-7pm
& Saturday 2-8pm.

This program is supported, in part, by public funds from the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs and The National Endowment for the Arts.

Issue 41 was collaboratively edited by Dana Faconti, Gemma Hart Ingalls, Jodie Vicenta Jacobson, and Glynnis McDaris, and features work by Davide Balula, Joe Deal, Constance DeJong, John Divola, Dru Donovan, Chris Gentile, Rachel Harrison, Corin Hewitt, David La Spina, Elad Lassry, Babette Mangolte, Ted Partin, Thomas Roma, Angela Strassheim, Padraig Timoney, Sara VanDerBeek, and Susanne M. Winterling.
