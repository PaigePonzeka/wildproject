Theatre ThrowDown

showtimes & tickets:

September 21 @ 8PM

Think Def Jam poetry slam meets a ten minute play festival and then they both go for a beer. Theatre companies from across the five kingdoms (borroughs) were painstakingly selected for their valor and honor and ability to throw down the friggin' gauntlet. Thus entrusted, they were given TWO WEEKS and a writing prompt to make an original ten minute play. Who will reign supreme?!?! Presented by the The Brooklyn Actor's Troupe and the Wild Project.
