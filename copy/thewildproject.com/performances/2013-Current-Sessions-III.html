the CURRENT SESSIONS
Volume III, Issue II

The CURRENT SESSIONS is a contemporary dance series which presents works from choreographers in NYC and beyond. Volume III, Issue II. will feature 10 choreographers and 1 dance film in 4 diverse programs. Fresh, raw, experimental, and curious, the CURRENT SESSIONS offers a glimpse of what dance is right now, and what it will look like in the future.
