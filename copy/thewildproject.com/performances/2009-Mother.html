Mother
  • Written by Lisa Ebersole
  • Directed by Andrew Grosso
  • Produced by Andrew Cahill

MOTHER is set at a throwback West Virginia resort over the New Year's holiday, where one family's gathering is anything but traditional. Infidelity, sibling rivalry, and a dash of kidnapping are all in an evening's diversion for this eccentric clan. BUCK HENRY will portray the father opposite HOLLAND TAYLOR's mother. The cast will also feature Ms. Ebersole as the daughter and Haskell King as the brother.
