GENDEROSITY

Creator/Writer/Performer- Donald C Shorter Jr
Co Executive Producer- Jeff Cleveland
Director- Matthew Mohr

GENDEROSITY is a one person show that examines the drag transformation process of artist Donald C. Shorter Jr into Francesca, his fabulous drag alter ego. This 60 min theatrical experience uses live vocals, lip synching, story telling, and video projections to discuss how non conforming to societal gender norms has affected his life in and out of the heels. The show exposes how this transformative process evolves in his life and has been a journey to self acceptance.
