the CURRENT SESSIONS
Volume III, Issue I

Produced by Alexis Convento

Photo credit: Corey Melton
Choreographer: Nathan Madden
Dancers: Ramona Kelley + Reed Luplau

showtimes:

March 1–3, 2013
Performance schedule: Friday & Saturday @ 7:30 PM; Sunday @ 3 PM with TalkBack & 6:30 PM

The CURRENT SESSIONS is a contemporary dance series which presents works from choreographers in NYC and beyond. Volume III, Issue I. will feature 16 choreographers and 2 dance films in 4 diverse programs. Fresh, raw, experimental, and curious, the CURRENT SESSIONS offers a glimpse of what dance is right now, and what it will look like in the future.
