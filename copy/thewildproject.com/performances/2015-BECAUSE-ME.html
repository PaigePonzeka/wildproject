28-year-old Else Barnes works at Trader Joe's and creates artwork from discarded yogurt cartons. Observing Else's life over the course of nine months as she navigates living alone for the first time, BECAUSE ME explores the ethos and behavior of Millennial's stumbling into adulthood.
