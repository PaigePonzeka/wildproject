SOMEDAY

Written & Directed by Cyrus Aaron
Produced by The Black Hours
Sound & Stage Manager A Creative Production
Light: Andrew Trent
Vocal Direction: Jeanette Berry

Featuring: Tyler Egan, Charity Phalo-Davis, Ryan Johnson, Jessie Lanza, Jasmine Lawrence, Justin Moore, Youdlyn Moreau, Michael Oloyede, Michelle Persoff, Briana Swann-Christie, Keith Benn Jr.

A multi-textured production composed of vignettes and spoken word narration shedding light on the pain and psychological plight of being a black man in America.

The Roman poet Ovid said, “Be patient and tough; someday this pain will be useful to you.”

But what will run out first, pain or time? #Somedaymustcome
