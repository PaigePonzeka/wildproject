Studio 42 Presents

A New Play by Adam Szymkowicz

My Base and Scurvy Heart

Directed by Moritz von Stuelpnagel

Featuring Ryan Andes*, Brian Belcinski, Amy Landon*, Christina Shipp, Sandra Struthers-Clerc* and Liz Wisan*

*appearing courtesy of Actor’s Equity Association

synopsis:

Few are more fearsome than Terrible Pirate Lottie, Scourge of Every Sea – with a Heart as Steely as her Sword and Beauty that Rivals her Bloodlust. Nary a man has been able to penetrate her Murderous Crew of Lesbian Rogues. But can she fend off both the Soft-Skinned Shepherdess with a Secret as well as the Rapacious Rival Rapscallion who threatens to Plunder her Booty? Studio 42 sets sail on a high seas voyage featuring Swashbuckling Swordfights! Pithy Parrots!! Lusty Wenches plotting Rousing Rendezvous!!! And a guaranteed heARRRty adventure!!!!
