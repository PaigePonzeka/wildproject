The Gerry Party

JOIN THE GERRY PARTY!
HAVE FUN AND IMPROVE YOUR LIFE!

Do you need more money?
Would you like to meet someone special?
...Are you tired, bored?
DO YOU LIKE TO HAVE FUN AND PARTY?
The answer is YES.

synopsis:

Join us for The Gerry Party, a show blending theater and television since it will be videotaped for a TV pilot.

This new variety show debut will feature Gerry Visco as emcee, and a cast of her crazy fabulous friends and enemies, a bunch of the most talented performers, artists, writers, go go boys/girls, club kids, drag queens, and personalities plucked from the vibrant nightlife of New York City throbbing right there on the stage or walking around in costume. Surprise guests, many of them insane, will be announced. Comedy, song, dance, talk, Gerry Visco, and YOU. You the audience is the party and YOU will be part of the show, sort of like Solid Gold, Ali G, Laugh In, Let's Make A Deal, In Living Color, and other shows with fun people.

Nutso DJ/performer Tyler Stone
Executive Producer Cole Nahal,
Performers Joseph Keckler, Gio Black Peter, Gabe Fuller The Virgin Queen, and lots of others, including YOU.

The Gerry Party is guaranteed to permanently alter your DNA and brain cells.

This is gonna be a PARTY!
