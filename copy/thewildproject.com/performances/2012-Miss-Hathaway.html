"The Mad World of Miss Hathaway"
All-Star Songbook Show!

Written by & Starring
Angela Di Carlo

After two sold out shows of The Mad World of Miss Hathaway: #5 Misogyny and Mistletoe in December and before presenting the eagerly anticipated 6th installment at The Wild Project in May, Angela Di Carlo (Just to be Polite at Joe's Pub, Ange Rampage at The Triad) is presenting a concert featuring the most memorable songs of the 5 past episodes of her hilarious original musical comedy, The Mad World of Miss Hathaway. The show is a musical parody of the TV drama, Mad Men, and was chosen by Time Out New York as a critics pick, featured in the Village Voice as a Voice Choice, and written up in Showbusinessweekly.com, Odyessy and Gayletter. It has quickly developed a devoted cult following among many fans of Mad Men and non- fans as well.

For this very special show, Ms. Di Carlo is joined by an all-star ensemble of downtown musical heavy hitters including: Amber Martin (Amber Alert!), Adam Dugas, (Chaos & Candy, The Citizens Band) Molly Pope, (Our Hit Parade) Bradford Scobie, (Moisty the Snowman), Kyle Supley, (Vintage Vantage Show) and a few special surprise guests as well. All performing the catchiest and most popular songs from the past episodes. With an expanded 3 piece band, “The 4 Martini Lunches” led by Kyle Forester (Ladybug Transistor). Come see who gets caught by the water cooler with their drink on and pants down! It's an evening of musical comedy not to be missed!
