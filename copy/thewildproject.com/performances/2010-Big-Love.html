The Studio/New York Conservatory presents

Big Love

By Charles L. Mee
Directed by Mike Donahue

Fifty sisters flee arranged marriages to their fifty cousins. Desperate for asylum, they discover an otherworldly villa-- complete with cupcakes, karaoke and cross-dressing. But when the jilted suitors show up to claim their brides, the ultimate battle of the sexes ignites in this hilarious, dark and dirty play about stumbling into true love.
