SATURN (a play about food)

In pursuit of new life, the young inheritors of a farm must piece together the past after a handful of extraordinary seeds puts the fate of the land and their marriage at stake. Riffing on the myth of Cronos, Jack and the Beanstalk and contemporary agricultural conundrums, SATURN is the fifth in a series of Planet Plays exploring the intersection between environmentalism and theater. 

Created by Superhero Clubhouse
Directed by Sarah Hughes
Written by Jeremy Pickard
Performed by Eva von Schweinitz, Tyler Caffall, Tom Coiner*, Ava Eisenson* & Maria Portman Kelly*
Sound Design by Eva von Schweinitz
Lighting Design by Alejandro Fajardo
Set/Costume Design by R.B. Schlather
Stage Managed by Virginia Leary
Assistant Directed by Marguerite French
Dramaturgy by Megan McClain

*These actors are appearing courtesy of Actors’ Equity Association.
SATURN is an Equity Approved Showcase.
