Notice Me

By Blair Singer
directed by: Sofia Alvarez
with Jake Green, Annabel Lalonde, Jason Shelton and Susan Spratt

Four teenagers in the San Fernando Valley are desperate to be noticed in the morass of **our contemporary 24-7 media culture. Like sound in a vacuum, their voices cry out unheard in the barren landscape of suburbia.
