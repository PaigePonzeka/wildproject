The CURRENT SESSIONS
Volume VI, Issue II: MOVEMENT CURRENCY

showtimes & tickets:

Friday, August 19 @ 7:30pm
Saturday, August 20 @ 7:30pm
Sunday, August 21 @ 3pm
Sunday, August 21 @ 7pm

For the 11th edition of the CURRENT SESSIONS’ biannual performance series, artists across contemporary movement will present works as part of Volume VI, Issue II: MOVEMENT CURRENCY, held Friday, August 19 to Sunday, August 21, 2016 at the wild project.

MOVEMENT CURRENCY will focus on how bodies produce, exchange, and perform within systems of value, drawing on figurative and ethnographic practices to consider the lineage of choreographies which qualify our social identity. Presented as four distinct programs: Risk, Debt, Savings, and Credit, MOVEMENT CURRENCY incorporates somatic and improvisational practices, as well as works examining personal movement histories, blackness, queerness, and cultural memory.

PROGRAMMING SCHEDULE

01 RISK // Friday, August 19, 2016 at 7:30pm
From fragility to preservation, harm to survival, an evening of work developing risk as a performative dimension which trades personal safety for choreographic opportunity. With artists Amanda Hunt, Ben Hagari (film), Bobbi Jene Smith, Jessica Pretty, and Katrina Reid.

02 DEBT // Saturday, August 20, 2016 at 7:30pm
Choreographing obligation, dances articulating indebtedness to history and those which play on debts of expectation. With artists Amanda Hunt, Brendan Drake, Gregory Holt, Jasmine Hearn (film), Tara Aisha Willis.

03 SAVINGS // Sunday, August 21, 2016 at 3:00pm with Talkback
When the dance is done, what remains? Toward a performative archive for memory, ancestry, and ritual. With artists Ben Hagari (film), Bobbi Jene Smith, Fana Fraser, Gregory Holt, Katrina Reid.

04 CREDIT // Sunday, August 21, 2016 at 7:00pm
In search of movement provenance. Works acknowledging impression and influence, tracing lineage and locating cultural ownership. With artists Brendan Drake, Fana Fraser, Jasmine Hearn (film), Jessica Pretty, Tara Aisha Willis.
