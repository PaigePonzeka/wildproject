HAUS: AN OPEN HOUSE + FUNDRAISER

Join the Team and Artists of the CURRENT SESSIONS at our first annual open house + fundraiser, HAUS. By DAY, participate or observe a workshop at our LAB // SESSIONS; at NIGHT, mingle with our artists, participate in our raffle (where you can win swag from local East Village businesses!), and celebrate the launch of our Indiegogo 2014 campaign! Come out and lend your support to our project, the artists we present, and the development of our SESSIONS in the coming year!

TIMELINE OF EVENT

DAY | 12p-3p | interact

LAB | SESSION #1 (12p-1:30p)
with Chihiro Shimizu, Artistic Director of CHIHIRO SHIMIZU and ARTISTS

LAB | SESSION #2 (1:30p-3p)
with Allison Jones, Co-Creative Director of the CURRENT SESSIONS and Artistic Director of Allison Jones Dance

NIGHT | 6p-9p | invest

MEET | GREET (6p-7p)
Meet the Team, our Artists and see the Venue

HAUS TALK (7p-8p)
Join in on a conversation about today’s dance landscape

FUND + LAUNCH (7p-9p)
Win swag from local East Village Businesses, mingle with our Artists + support our Indiegogo campaign launch for 2014.
