THE MAD WORLD OF MISS HATHAWAY: COUNTER CULTURE CRISIS-MESS

Written by & Starring
Angela Di Carlo
with
Mike Albo, Robert Appleton, Steven Ferezy, David Ilku, Jill Pangallo, Stephen Michael Rondell, Clayton Dean Smith Ede Thurrell & Gia Mele
MUSICAL ACCOMPANIMENT BY KYLE FORESTER

THE RETURN OF BOOB-TUBE INSPIRED MUSICAL PARODY, "THE MAD WORLD OF MISS HATHAWAY: “COUNTER CULTURE CRISIS-MESS!"

It's Christmas time in 1969 and everything is NOT fine at Spencer Colfax Raper Shenanigans Hathaway Clutter Gayson and Chunk advertising agency! When the office is rocked by shocking personnel changes that jeopardize the future of the company, know-it-all advertising maven JoAnne Hathaway springs into action trying to keep the office jolly and the clients grooving In the tumultuous time of 1969, where change is the norm and everyone is on drugs...even at Christmas time! A hilarious holiday musical roller-coaster ride of a show not to missed!

Music, lyrics and book written by and starring Angela Di Carlo (The Downtown Series at Feinstein's, Just to be Polite at Joe's Pub) as JoAnne Hathaway.

With a hilarious all-star supporting cast including Mike Albo (The Junket), Clayton Dean Smith (Mr. Irresistible), David Ilku (The Dueling Bankheads, Unitard) Jill Pangallo (Hope Is Expensive, The Ho Ho's), Stephen Michael Rondel (The New Acting Company, Muffins in the Window) and more. With Kyle Forester (Ladybug Transistor, Crystal Stilts) on piano.

"The Mad World of Miss Hathaway” has been featured in the “Lowbrow/Brilliant” section of New York Magazine's infamous Approval Matrix and was chosen by Time Out New York as a critics pick, featured in the Village Voice as a Voice Choice, and written up in New York Press and Capitol New York. After eleven hilarious episodes, it has quickly developed a devoted cult following among many fans of Mad Men (and non-fans as well!).
